Why I Bake

I started baking just 3 years ago. It gave me a way to slow down and be present. There's an exactness to baking that I really love, and it's so satisfying to create something delicious.

I made this website to share the recipes I come back to time and time again, with honest tips on what to watch out for and how to make each one a little more special.
